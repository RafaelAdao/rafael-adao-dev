_This article was originally published on [Substack](https://rafaeladao.substack.com/p/notebooklm-gerou-um-podcast-sobre) (including the podcast episode player)._

_And this is what happened…_

**Audio:** the podcast episode is still available on the [original Substack post](https://rafaeladao.substack.com/p/notebooklm-gerou-um-podcast-sobre)—the direct audio URL there is temporary (signed), so I did not embed a player here.

I used [NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com/) to generate a podcast about my post [“Notes with Zettelkasten”](/en/2023/05/08/anotacoes-com-zettelkasten/) and loved the result. Read the post, then listen to what the model produced. I was impressed by the audio quality and the back-and-forth between the two voices. It reminded me of Jon Radoff’s [*Metavert Meditations*](https://meditations.metavert.io/) article [“The Direct from Imagination Era Has Begun”](https://meditations.metavert.io/p/the-direct-from-imagination-era-has-begun-8a01244b75), which [introduces](https://meditations.metavert.io/i/104120940/who-will-be-disrupted) the chart below on where generative AI may be most disruptive.

![](/images/posts/222a973c-7a4b-4bae-b426-8a7e00df4fba_800x480.png)

I won’t argue whether those industries will actually be reshaped—many people still prefer content that is verifiably 100% human-made.
