_This article was originally published on [Substack](https://rafaeladao.substack.com/p/descubra-recursos-de-aprendizagem)._

I want to share the “Zettelkasten and Note Taking Learning Resources” repository I created to gather and share material on note-taking and Zettelkasten—a note organization method that can boost learning, creative thinking, and content production.

![](/images/posts/8617e8e2-8bfb-4b8f-b763-057247234b1f_1280x640.jpeg)

In the repo you’ll find a curated set of links to learn more about Zettelkasten, from articles to videos. Highlights:

- **Articles:** [English translation of Niklas Luhmann (1981): *Kommunikation mit Zettelkästen*](https://zettelkasten.de/communications-with-zettelkastens/)
- **Recommended books:**

  1. [Getting Started With an Antinet Zettelkasten](https://www.scottscheper.com/free-guide)
  2. [Antinet Zettelkasten: A Knowledge System That Will Turn You Into a Prolific Reader, Researcher and Writer](https://www.scottscheper.com/antinet)
  3. [How to Take Smart Notes: One Simple Technique to Boost Writing, Learning and Thinking](https://www.soenkeahrens.de/en/takesmartnotes)

- **Videos:**

  - [The process of writing *World History of Design*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Kxyy0THLfuI)
  - [*Antinet* Zettelkasten Book Club Meeting](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AVma3xZg0gQ)
  - [Scott P. Scheper’s channel](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bP7AXjA4O6U&list=PLSofW8L-FnU-Z8BFXqv8JZt5Cp0mj2_Eb) — author of *Antinet*

Explore these resources and connect with other Zettelkasten enthusiasts. The repository is open to contributions—feel free to add or improve entries.

Repository: [GitHub — Zettelkasten and Note Taking Learning Resources](https://github.com/RafaelAdao/zettelkasten-and-note-taking-learning-resources).

I hope this helps you sharpen your note-taking and knowledge organization. Share it with anyone who might care!
